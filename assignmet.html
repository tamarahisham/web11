Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quik dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables,or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation : 5 minutes

  • Cooking : 5 minutes


    ingredients

  • 2-3 laege eggs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper,to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

    Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are

    wali mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.

    2. Heat the pon: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

    3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted tond bubbling, pour in the eggs. Till the

    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surfoce.

    4.Add filings (optional): When the eggs begin to set of the edges but slightly

    rnurry in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.

    5.Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift une edge und fold it

    over the fillngs. Let it cook, for anather minute, then slide it onto a plate.

    6.Enjay: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
